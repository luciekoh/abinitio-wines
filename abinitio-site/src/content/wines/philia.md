I never thought I'd tell our story through a wine, but here we are. Philia was born in the vineyard one winter, pruning the old Folle Blanche vines in a beating December rain — under my oilskin I remembered: you used to open the oysters in the garage sink and as a small child at waist height I took that wave of iodine straight in the face.
